Classic Share-Grammar Lab

Card chrondle-ux-share-unification · 2026-07-07 · 8 structurally distinct grammars for the Classic daily share text. Delegated-verdict mode: winner chosen and implemented; operator may re-verdict.

Contract every option must satisfy
  • Express the range mechanic: final range width and contained vs. missed — not merely hints used.
  • Spoiler-safe: no target year, no era (BC/AD), no range endpoints, no hint content. Widths and miss distances are relative and non-derivable.
  • Format family: Chrondle #N header · body · https://chrondle.app footer (shared with Order and Duel).
Fixtures: W = win, 21-year range, 3 hints, 92/100 · L = miss, 30-year range, 4 hints, 34y early.

Winner · Option B

Timeline Band + Target Marker

Rationale. B is the only option where the viral visual element itself carries the whole mechanic honestly: band length is the width you committed to, the 🎯 marker inside or outside the band is containment, and the marker's side is the miss direction — all from data the game actually records (unlike H's fabricated intermediate rows). It survives proportional fonts (pure emoji, unlike F/D's alignment-dependent art), keeps a scannable grid identity in a feed (unlike E), and stays spoiler-safe because the strip has no anchored scale — a viewer learns width and verdict, never the year or era. Hints and score remain as secondary stat lines, so nothing the current share communicates is lost.

Locked spec. 10-cell strip; band length clamp(round(1 + 7·ln(width)/ln(250)), 1, 8); contained → green band centered with 🎯 at its center cell; missed → amber band flushed away from the miss side, 🎯 placed 1 cell beyond the band edge (2 cells when the miss distance exceeds 25 years). Stat line 📏 W years · 🔍 H hints (each part singular-aware, hint part omitted at 0); score line keeps the existing emoji ladder, or 🫠 Dy early/late on a miss.
